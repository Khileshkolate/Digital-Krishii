Description

Planting Onion seeds can be planted in late July to September using the seed-to-seed method, or in September to October using the bulb-to-seed method.

Pollination Onion seeds need to be pollinated, usually by honeybees, in clear, warm weather with temperatures between 65°F and 95°F.

Harvesting Mature seeds are usually harvested by hand or machine from mid June to August of the following year.

Drying The stalks are laid out on tarps and dried for 2-3 weeks.

Processing The umbels are thrashed using combines, and the seed is then cleaned and processed.
