Contract Farming Formats

This contract specifies the quality, quantity, and price at which the crops will be purchased by the buyer, ensuring market alignment.

In this format, the buyer provides essential resources such as seeds, fertilizers, and technical guidance to the farmer for the production process.

This contract involves the buyer offering management assistance for the production process, ensuring that the product meets specific standards.

In this contract, the buyer leases land from the farmer for crop production, often providing resources or technical know-how for cultivation.

This contract guarantees that the buyer will purchase the entire output of the farmer's crop at a pre-agreed price, reducing market risk for the farmer.

In a joint-venture contract, both the buyer and the farmer share the profits, risks, and responsibilities of the farming operation.
